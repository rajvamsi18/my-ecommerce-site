Delivery Information

Delivery Areas

We currently offer delivery to all postcodes within a 20-mile radius of our store locations. To check if we deliver to your area, enter your postcode during checkout or contact our customer service team.

Delivery Times

We offer flexible delivery slots to fit your schedule:

  • Monday to Friday: 8am-10am, 10am-12pm, 12pm-2pm, 2pm-4pm, 4pm-6pm, 6pm-8pm
  • Saturday: 8am-10am, 10am-12pm, 12pm-2pm, 2pm-4pm
  • Sunday: 10am-12pm, 12pm-2pm, 2pm-4pm

Same-Day Delivery

Orders placed before 1pm Monday through Saturday may be eligible for same-day delivery, subject to slot availability. This service is available in select postcodes only.

Delivery Fees

Order Value Delivery Fee
Under £30 £4.99
£30 - £50 £2.99
Over £50 FREE

Delivery Process

  1. Order Placement: Place your order through our website or mobile app.
  2. Order Confirmation: You'll receive an email confirming your order details and selected delivery slot.
  3. Order Processing: Our team carefully selects your items, focusing on freshness and quality.
  4. Dispatch: Your order is packed with care, keeping cold items separate from ambient products.
  5. On Its Way: You'll receive a notification when your order is out for delivery.
  6. Delivery: Our friendly driver will deliver your groceries right to your door.

Special Instructions

During checkout, you can add special delivery instructions, such as:

  • Leave with a neighbor
  • Place in a safe location
  • Call upon arrival
  • Delivery to a specific entrance

Contactless Delivery

We offer contactless delivery for all orders. Simply select this option at checkout, and our driver will leave your order at your doorstep, ring the bell, and step back to maintain a safe distance.

Substitutions Policy

If an item in your order is unavailable, we may offer a suitable alternative. You can:

  • Accept all substitutions (we'll always try to match or upgrade)
  • Review substitutions upon delivery (accept or reject)
  • Opt for no substitutions during checkout

Questions?

If you have any questions about our delivery service, please contact our customer service team or call us at (555) 123-4567.
