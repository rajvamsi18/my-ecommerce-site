Returns Policy

Our Quality Guarantee

At FreshMart, we stand behind the quality of everything we sell. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Fresh or Money Back

We guarantee the freshness of all our products. If you're not happy with the quality of any item, we'll refund you in fullâ€”no questions asked.

Return Policy by Product Category

Fresh Produce, Meat, Seafood, and Dairy

These perishable items can be returned within 48 hours of delivery if they:

  • Are not fresh upon delivery
  • Are damaged or spoiled
  • Do not meet our quality standards

Simply contact our customer service team with your order number and photos of the items (if possible).

Packaged Foods and Household Items

Unopened, non-perishable items can be returned within 14 days of delivery if they:

  • Are damaged or defective
  • Were incorrectly described online
  • Are not what you ordered

Non-Food Items

Unopened non-food items (e.g., kitchen gadgets, cleaning supplies) can be returned within 30 days of delivery.

Items Not Eligible for Return

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened food products (for food safety reasons)
  • Personal care items that have been opened or used
  • Gift cards and promotional codes
  • Clearance items marked as "final sale"

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact Us: Call our customer service at (555) 123-4567 or email returns@freshmart.com within the applicable return period.
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number, the items you wish to return, and the reason for return. Photos of damaged items are helpful but not required.
  3. Return Authorization: You'll receive a return authorization and instructions.
  4. Return Options:
    • In-store returns at any FreshMart location (fastest option for refund)
    • Free return pickup during your next delivery (must be scheduled)
    • Return shipping (for non-perishable items only)

Refund Process

Refunds are processed as follows:

  • In-store returns: Immediate refund to original payment method or store credit
  • Return pickup/shipping: Refund issued within 3-5 business days after we receive and process your return

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase. If that's not possible, we'll issue store credit.

Damaged During Delivery

If an item is damaged during delivery, please:

  1. Document the damage with photos if possible
  2. Contact customer service within 24 hours of delivery
  3. Keep the damaged item and packaging until your claim is resolved

Wrong Items Delivered

If you receive the wrong item, please contact us within 48 hours. We will arrange for the correct item to be delivered and pick up the incorrect item at no additional cost.

Customer Satisfaction

Our goal is your complete satisfaction. If you have any issues with your order that aren't covered by this policy, please contact our customer service team, and we'll work with you to make it right.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our returns policy, please contact our customer service team or call us at (555) 123-4567.
