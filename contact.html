Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all addresses within a 20-mile radius of our store locations. Enter your postcode on our delivery page to check if we deliver to your area.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery is free for orders over £50. For orders under £50, a delivery fee of £3.99 applies.

When will my order arrive?

Orders placed before 1 pm are eligible for same-day delivery (select areas only). All other orders will be delivered the next day during your selected time slot.
