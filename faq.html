Ordering

How do I place an order?

Placing an order with FreshMart is easy:

  1. Browse our products or use the search function to find what you need
  2. Add items to your cart
  3. Click "Checkout" when you're ready
  4. Select your delivery slot and enter your address
  5. Choose your payment method
  6. Confirm your order

You'll receive an email confirmation once your order is placed.

What is the minimum order value?

There is no minimum order value, but orders under £30 have a delivery fee of £4.99. Orders between £30-£50 have a reduced delivery fee of £2.99, and orders over £50 qualify for free delivery.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order up to 12 hours before your scheduled delivery time. To make changes:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Select the order you wish to modify
  4. Click "Modify Order" or "Cancel Order"

If it's less than 12 hours before delivery, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Delivery

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all postcodes within a 20-mile radius of our store locations. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your postcode during checkout or on our Delivery Information page.

What are your delivery hours?

We offer delivery during the following times:

  • Monday to Friday: 8am-8pm (in 2-hour slots)
  • Saturday: 8am-4pm (in 2-hour slots)
  • Sunday: 10am-4pm (in 2-hour slots)

You can select your preferred delivery slot during checkout.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 1pm Monday through Saturday, subject to slot availability. This service is currently available in select postcodes only. You'll see available same-day slots during checkout if your address is eligible.

How much does delivery cost?

Our delivery fees are as follows:

  • Orders under £30: £4.99
  • Orders between £30-£50: £2.99
  • Orders over £50: FREE

Products & Pricing

How do you ensure product freshness?

Freshness is our top priority. We:

  • Source produce directly from local farms whenever possible
  • Receive daily deliveries from our suppliers
  • Have strict quality control processes
  • Select items for your order on the day of delivery
  • Transport products in temperature-controlled vehicles

All our fresh products come with a freshness guarantee—if you're not satisfied, we'll refund you in full.

Are your prices the same as in-store?

Yes, our online prices match our in-store prices. We occasionally run online-exclusive promotions, which will be clearly marked on our website.

Do you offer organic products?

Yes, we offer a wide range of certified organic products across all departments. You can filter for organic products using our search filters, or browse our dedicated organic section.

What if an item I ordered is out of stock?

If an item in your order is unavailable, we'll either:

  • Substitute it with a similar or higher quality item (at the lower price if the substitute is more expensive)
  • Remove it from your order and refund you

You can set your substitution preferences during checkout for each order or for your account overall.

Payment & Discounts

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay (on compatible devices)
  • Google Pay (on compatible devices)
  • FreshMart gift cards

We do not accept cash on delivery.

When will I be charged for my order?

We authorize your payment method when you place your order, but you'll only be charged once your order is dispatched for delivery. The final amount will reflect any adjustments due to product substitutions, weight variations for fresh items, or items that were unavailable.

Do you offer any discounts or loyalty programs?

Yes, we offer several ways to save:

  • FreshMart Rewards: Earn points on every purchase that can be redeemed for discounts
  • Weekly Specials: Save on selected items each week
  • Bulk Discounts: Save when buying eligible items in larger quantities
  • Newsletter Promotions: Exclusive offers for our newsletter subscribers

Sign up for our newsletter or create an account to start saving today!

Account & Technical

Do I need an account to shop with you?

Yes, you need to create an account to place an order. This allows us to store your delivery address, payment information (securely), order history, and preferences to make future shopping faster and more convenient.

How do I create an account?

To create an account:

  1. Click "My Account" in the top navigation
  2. Select "Create Account"
  3. Fill in your details and create a password
  4. Verify your email address through the link we'll send you

Once verified, you can start shopping right away!

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

To reset your password:

  1. Click "My Account" in the top navigation
  2. Select "Sign In"
  3. Click "Forgot Password"
  4. Enter your email address
  5. Follow the instructions in the email we'll send you

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption for all sensitive information, never store complete payment card details on our servers, and have strict data protection policies in place. You can review our full privacy policy for more details.

Returns & Refunds

What is your returns policy?

We stand behind the quality of all our products:

  • Fresh items: If not fresh upon delivery, report within 48 hours
  • Packaged foods: Unopened items can be returned within 14 days
  • Non-food items: Unopened items can be returned within 30 days

Please visit our Returns Policy page for complete details.

How do I report an issue with my order?

If you have any issues with your order, please:

  1. Contact our customer service team at (555) 123-4567
  2. Email us at customercare@freshmart.com
  3. Use the "Report Issue" button in your order details in your account

Please have your order number ready and, if possible, take photos of any damaged items.

How long do refunds take to process?

Refund processing times depend on your payment method:

  • Credit/debit cards: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal: 1-2 business days
  • Store credit: Immediate

Note that while we process refunds promptly, your bank or payment provider may have additional processing times.
